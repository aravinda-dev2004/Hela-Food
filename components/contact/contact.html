Frequently Asked Questions

We deliver Monday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Orders placed before 2:00 PM are typically delivered the same day.

We currently deliver throughout Colombo and surrounding areas. Enter your postal code at checkout to verify delivery availability in your location.

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order, contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement.

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your order by logging into your account.

Many of our products are certified organic. Look for the "Organic" label on product pages. We work directly with local farmers who use sustainable farming practices.
